#### Room Adventure
**Room Adventure** is a game about an average salaryman passing away in his sleep and having his body inhabited
by an outer being. As this outer being your goal is simple, leave this average apartment filled with average things
as an average man and cause utter chaos. A simple task for you normally... but your strength is sealed... and the door
... is locked.

### Rooms
- **Room One** - Contains: Chair, Desk. Bed. Items: Key.
- **Room Two** - Contains: Fireplace, Rug, Couch. Items: Coal.
- **Room Three** - Contains: Toilet, Bathtub. Items: NONE.
- **Room Four** - Contains: Door, Rack. Items: Coat.

### Controls
Based on what you type you can do various actions:
  - **Go** [ROOM] - Takes you from your current Room into a room adjacent to it.
  - **Look** [ITEM] - Allows you to investigate the items that can be found in a room.
  - **Take** [ITEM] - Allows you to take an item to add to your inventory.
  - **Use** [ITEM1] [ITEM2] - Allows you to use one item on another item.
